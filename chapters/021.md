## Day 21 – Stay With Me Until Joy

> *No cross carried with Christ ends anywhere but joy.*

I was shown that there are times when love asks us not to explain suffering, but simply to remain with Christ in it. As I gazed upon His Passion, my heart was filled with grief and compassion. I thought I was watching the final moments before all life would leave Him. I expected only darkness. Yet just as sorrow seemed to have reached its deepest point, everything changed. Without warning, His countenance became radiant with joy, and my own heart was transformed with His. Then He gently asked me, "Where now is your pain? Where now is your grief?" In His presence, sorrow gave way so completely that it was as though it had never possessed the final word.

I understood then that this is also the pattern of your own life. For now, you walk with Christ beneath the weight of the cross. You know weariness, disappointment, unanswered questions, and grief. There are days when hope feels hidden beneath the burden you carry. Yet do not imagine that this road ends in loss. Stay with Him. Remain where love has placed you. The One who shares your suffering will also share His joy with you. The distance between the cross and resurrection is shorter than you can imagine, though while you are walking it, it often feels long.

There are moments when I wish you could see yourself as Christ already sees you. If He were to unveil the fullness of His joy even for an instant, every fear would lose its power. Every sorrow would be swallowed up in wonder. You would discover that beneath all the changing circumstances of your life, divine joy has been quietly holding you from the beginning. Yet for now, He gives you only what your soul can bear. He does not hide His glory to withhold it from you, but to gently prepare your heart to receive it without being overwhelmed.

So do not despise the small sufferings that accompany love, nor imagine they have no place in the life of God. Every burden carried with Christ enlarges your capacity for joy. Every act of faithfulness, every quiet endurance, every tear offered in trust is shaping your soul for a glory that cannot yet be fully seen. One day Christ will look upon you with the same radiant countenance that I was shown, and every grief will vanish into that gaze. Then you will understand that nothing endured with Him was ever wasted. Even the smallest cross, carried in love, has been preparing you for an endless knowledge of the God whose joy will forever eclipse every sorrow.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I remain with You.*

> Exhale: *Lead me into joy.*
