## Day 85 – It Is Well

> *One day you will see that Love was faithful in every moment you could not yet understand.*

As I pondered all that had been shown to me, I found myself filled with quiet wonder. Here we walk through life with such limited sight. We misunderstand, we forget, we stumble, and we often cannot see what God is doing. Yet none of this troubles Him as it troubles us. Our gracious Lord beholds the whole of our journey with perfect joy and unwavering peace. He knows its beginning, its middle, and its glorious end all at once. What pleases Him most is not that we understand everything, but that we trust His heart. Faith delights Him because it allows us to rejoice with Him before we yet see what He already sees.

I was shown that your life did not begin with your birth. Long before you drew your first breath, you were already held within the loving knowledge of God. Before the foundations of the world, His heart rested upon you. It was from this eternal love that He created you, and by this same love He continues to sustain you every day. There has never been a moment when you have slipped beyond His care. Even through your weakness, your sorrow, and your wandering, He has guarded the deepest purpose for which He made you. Nothing that truly belongs to your everlasting joy can finally be lost.

There are many questions that remain hidden from us now. You have surely asked them yourself. Why this sorrow? Why this delay? Why this loss? Why was one path opened and another closed? Our minds naturally try to gather these scattered pieces into a story we can understand. Yet I was shown that there is a wisdom beyond our present sight. One day the hidden threads will be revealed. What now seems unfinished will be seen as complete. What now appears confusing will shine with a beauty that only eternity can unveil. Not because suffering was good in itself, but because Love never ceased working within it.

When that day comes, we will no longer look back wishing the story had been written differently. We will not say, "Lord, if only this had happened instead." We will not imagine that we could have governed our lives more wisely than infinite Love. Every question will find its quiet answer, not through argument, but through vision. Standing in the fullness of God's presence, we will finally see what His love has been accomplishing all along. Wonder will replace regret. Gratitude will swallow every lingering complaint. Our hearts will rest because they will behold the wisdom that has guided every step.

Until that day, let your heart learn this simple prayer of trust. When you cannot understand, bless Him anyway. When the path disappears into mystery, continue walking in hope. When sorrow obscures your vision, remember that His vision has never been obscured. Live today in the confidence that the Love which knew you before time began is the same Love that carries you now and will welcome you home. And when all things are finally revealed, your own heart will join the everlasting song of heaven, saying with joy and without hesitation: **"Lord, blessed are You, for it is thus. It is well."**

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I trust Your wisdom.*

> Exhale: *It is well.*
