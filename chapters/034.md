## Day 34 – God Reveals What Love Requires

> *Love never withholds what the soul truly needs.*

Do not be troubled because there are things you cannot yet understand. There are mysteries that God keeps within His own wisdom until the proper time, and there are truths He delights to uncover little by little as your heart is able to receive them. Learn to distinguish between the two. Some doors remain closed for now because they belong to God's perfect counsel. Others only seem closed because your eyes are still learning to see. Be patient with yourself. The One who teaches you is infinitely patient.

Never imagine that God enjoys keeping you in ignorance. If something is necessary for your life with Him, He will faithfully reveal it. The Lord is never reluctant to give light. He does not guard truth like a treasure that must be earned. Rather, He is like a loving teacher who knows exactly when each lesson will bring life instead of confusion. What you need today will be given today. What belongs to tomorrow will come with tomorrow's grace. Trust the kindness of His timing as much as the generosity of His heart.

Whenever you grow in love, compassion, humility, or hope, you are already receiving His teaching. God is not only the One who gives wisdom. He is Wisdom itself. He is the beginning of every true desire, the path that carries you forward, the voice that gently calls your name, and the joy that awaits you at journey's end. Every glimpse of truth that awakens your heart is another invitation to know Him more deeply. As you draw near to Him, hidden things slowly become clear—not all at once, but as dawn quietly overtakes the night.

I was also shown that every revelation God gives is meant to strengthen hope, especially when we become aware of sin and sorrow. There are moments when you look honestly at yourself or at the brokenness of the world and wonder whether goodness can truly prevail. Yet the Lord does not leave you there. Again and again He speaks the same gracious promise: **All shall be well.** He does not deny the reality of sin, nor does He abandon you to it. His final word is always restoration. So receive with gratitude every truth He has already placed before you, and wait with confidence for every truth that love has not yet unveiled. The God who has begun to teach your heart will never cease until you know Him face to face.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Teach me Your way.*

> Exhale: *I trust Your timing.*
