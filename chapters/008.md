## Day 8 – As Though Shown to You

> *What God reveals in love is never meant for one heart alone.*

As I reflected upon all that had been shown to me, I began to see that every vision was held together by a single thread. I saw Christ's suffering, His mother's humble faith, the everlasting life of the Holy Trinity, the beauty of all creation, the faithful care by which every creature is sustained, and the goodness of God shining through all that is good. At first these seemed like many different revelations, but they were not separate at all. They were many windows opening onto the same endless love. The more I beheld God, the smaller everything else appeared—not because creation had lost its beauty, but because I had glimpsed the One from whom all beauty comes.

When your eyes are fixed only upon the world, its troubles can seem overwhelming. Your worries grow large. Your failures loom heavy. Even your successes can appear more important than they truly are. But when your heart is lifted toward God, everything finds its proper place. The world remains beautiful, yet it no longer bears the weight of being your ultimate hope. You begin to see that every good thing is a gift pointing beyond itself, inviting you toward the One who is goodness itself.

What surprised me most was that I did not wish to keep this joy for myself. Love cannot remain hidden. As my heart was filled, it immediately longed for others to see what I had seen and to discover the same comfort. This is the way of divine love. It does not make us possessive of spiritual gifts, as though they belonged to us alone. Instead, it enlarges the heart until every neighbor becomes someone we quietly hope will awaken to the goodness of God. The truest revelations are never given to make one person seem special. They are given so that all may be encouraged.

So I ask you not to dwell upon the one who received these visions, but upon the God who delights to give them. Receive these words as though Christ Himself had spoken them into your own heart, for His desire is not to comfort only one soul in one generation. His love reaches across every age with the same tenderness. What He revealed to me was always meant for you as well. Therefore, receive it with joy, not as the memory of another's encounter with God, but as an invitation to recognize the divine love that has already been seeking you all your life.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Your love is for me.*
>
> Exhale: *I receive it with joy.*
