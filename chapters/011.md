## Day 11 – Nothing Escapes Love

> *The hands that created the world have never ceased holding it.*

There came a moment when my understanding grew wonderfully still, and I saw that God is present in all things. It was not that everything is God, but that nothing exists apart from His sustaining presence. His wisdom reaches farther than I can see, and His love works more deeply than I can imagine. So much that appears random to us is not hidden from Him. We call events chance because our vision is small. Yet the One who sees the whole story has never lost sight of even the smallest thread.

As I rested in this vision, a question rose quietly within me: *What, then, is sin?* I had been shown only the goodness of God's own working, and nowhere did I see evil flowing from His hands. I understood that God is never the author of sin. Sin has no true life of its own. It creates nothing. It brings nothing into being. It is not a work like love is a work, or mercy is a work, or creation is a work. It is the turning away from the life God continually gives. It wounds, but it cannot overcome the goodness from which all things draw their being.

This understanding does not ask us to ignore the sorrow of the world or pretend that suffering is unreal. We know too well that hearts are broken, injustice is committed, and lives are marked by deep pain. Yet I was invited to look beyond the limits of human judgment and to trust that God never abandons His creation to meaninglessness. The One who made all things in love has never withdrawn His hands from what He has made. Even where we cannot yet see the path, He continues to work patiently, wisely, and tenderly toward healing. His purpose is not fragile. His love is not interrupted.

So when you cannot make sense of your life, begin not with your own understanding but with God's faithfulness. Lift your eyes from fear to the One who says, "See—I am here. I am in all things. I am still at work." You will not always know how every sorrow will be redeemed, nor why every road has bent as it has. Some mysteries remain beyond our sight. But you may rest in this: the God who made you has never ceased caring for you. His love has not forgotten you for a single moment, and nothing can finally frustrate the good purpose that flows from His endless wisdom.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You are at work.*
>
> Exhale: *I rest in Your love.*
