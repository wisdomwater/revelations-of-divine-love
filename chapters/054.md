## Day 54 – Your Truest Home

> *You do not journey toward God from a place where He is absent; you awaken to the One in whom you have always dwelt.*

Because God's love is without measure, He does not divide His affection between souls as though some were more worthy of His nearness than others. The love with which He embraces Christ is the love through which He gathers every soul that will be made whole. This is a mystery beyond my understanding, yet I was shown enough to trust it: where Christ is, there His people are also. He has not merely gone ahead to prepare a place for you. He has drawn your life into His own. You are not loved from a distance, but held within the life of the Beloved Himself.

Therefore rejoice that God dwells within your soul. Yet let your joy grow even deeper, for there is something more wonderful still: your soul dwells in God. Your heart may imagine itself wandering through a vast and uncertain world, searching for a place of safety, but your deepest life has never been homeless. God has made your soul His dwelling, and He Himself is the dwelling of your soul. As the fish lives within the sea and the child rests within a mother's embrace, so your life is surrounded by the Life that gave it being. Even when you forget Him, you have never slipped outside His presence.

As I looked more deeply, I could scarcely speak of any distance between God and the substance of the soul. I did not see two realities standing apart from one another, but the creature continually upheld within its Creator. God alone is God, and we remain His beloved creatures, yet every breath of our existence is sustained within His own life. The Father holds you in His strength. The Son gathers you into His wisdom and redeeming love. The Holy Spirit fills you with divine goodness. You are enclosed within the Holy Trinity, and the Holy Trinity graciously dwells within you. This holy communion is nearer than thought and more constant than your heartbeat.

Faith begins here. It is more than agreeing with sacred words or striving to convince yourself of unseen things. Faith is the quiet awakening to what has always been true. It is the growing understanding, joined with steadfast trust, that you are in God and God is in you, though your eyes cannot yet behold Him as He is. This trust becomes the doorway through which every other grace enters. Love deepens. Hope strengthens. Courage rises. Patience flowers. Not because you manufacture these virtues, but because the life of Christ is already at work within you.

When you feel alone, remember where you truly live. When fear tells you that you have been abandoned, return gently to this hidden home. You are not held together by your own faithfulness but by the faithful presence of God within you. Every prayer, every longing, every quiet turning of your heart is already taking place inside the embrace of divine love. Rest there often. The more deeply you awaken to this mystery, the more your life will begin to reflect the peace of the One who has never ceased to make His home in you.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *You dwell in me.*

> **Exhale:** *I dwell in You.*
