## Day 10 – Seek, Wait, Trust

> *The heart that keeps seeking has already begun to find.*

As I gazed upon the face of Christ, I longed to see Him more clearly. What was given to me seemed only a glimpse, and my heart ached for a fuller vision. Then I understood something that has remained with me ever since: when God desires to reveal Himself, He Himself becomes our light. We do not discover Him by straining harder or seeing farther. We find Him because He first makes Himself known. Every desire to seek God is already a sign that His grace has quietly begun its work within us.

This is the rhythm of the soul. We catch a glimpse of God's goodness, and our longing deepens. We seek Him, and even while we seek, we discover that He has never truly left us. There are moments when His presence feels unmistakably near, and others when we seem to search through silence. Yet neither season is wasted. I learned that I could both have Him and still long for Him. Love is like this. It is never content to remain where it is but always reaches more deeply into the One who first loved us.

I was also shown that even when we cannot clearly see God, He is nearer than we imagine. It seemed to me that if a person were beneath the deepest waters of the sea, yet could behold God as He truly is, they would remain safe, whatever surrounded them. So it is with us. The storms of life are real, and there are days when the waters rise above our heads. Yet beneath every wave, beneath every fear, beneath every unanswered question, the faithful presence of God remains unchanged. We often think we are searching for Him while all along He has been quietly holding us.

So do not lose heart if your journey feels more like seeking than finding. Seeking is not a lesser form of faith. It is one of love's most beautiful expressions. God delights in the soul that continues to seek, continues to wait, and continues to trust. There is holy work even in the longing. Continue to seek with patience. Wait without bitterness. Trust without fear. The One who has hidden Himself for a time has not hidden His love. And when the moment is right, He will reveal Himself in ways more beautiful than you could have imagined. Until then, your faithful seeking is already pleasing to His heart.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I seek You.*
>
> Exhale: *I trust Your timing.*
