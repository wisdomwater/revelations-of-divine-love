## Day 50 – Held Even in Your Questions

> *The questions that draw you closer to God are already part of His answer.*

There are times when your heart holds two truths that seem impossible to reconcile. You know your failures. You see the ways you have wounded yourself and others, and you do not wish to pretend they are anything less than real. Yet when you turn toward God, you discover not accusation but love. You expect to find condemnation, yet you are met instead with mercy. I, too, stood within this holy tension. I knew that sin was no illusion, and yet whenever the Lord revealed Himself to me, I could not see blame in His gaze. His eyes rested upon me with a love that never wavered.

This mystery troubled me deeply. I did not want to deny the truth about human sin, nor did I wish to diminish the boundless goodness of God. I longed to understand how both could be true at once. My mind searched for an answer, but my heart found only wonder. Still, I did not turn away. I remained before the Lord with my questions, trusting that the One who is Truth need not fear an honest seeker. There is a humility that does not rush to resolve every mystery but waits patiently for God to reveal what the soul is ready to receive.

You may find yourself in that same place. There are seasons when faith feels less like possessing certainty and more like standing reverently before a mystery too beautiful to grasp. Do not be discouraged by this. The desire to understand can become an act of love when it keeps your eyes fixed upon God instead of turning them away from Him. Bring Him your questions. Offer Him your confusion. He is not offended by the longing of a heart that sincerely seeks the truth. Sometimes the greatest faith is not found in having every answer but in refusing to leave the presence of Love until light is given.

So do not measure God's heart by the voice of your own self-condemnation. Let Him teach you how He sees you. Be honest about your sin, yet even more honest about His mercy. Hold both truths gently until He joins them together in His own wisdom. There are answers that unfold only through faithful waiting. The Lord who has carried you this far will not leave your deepest questions unanswered forever. Continue to seek Him with courage and humility, and trust that every sincere longing for truth is already being held within the embrace of the One who is Truth itself.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *Lead me into truth.*

> **Exhale:** *I trust Your love.*
