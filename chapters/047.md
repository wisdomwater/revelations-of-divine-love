## Day 47 – When the Light Seems to Fade

> *Even when you cannot feel God's presence, His love has not withdrawn.*

There are moments when God seems wonderfully near. Your heart is quiet, your soul is filled with peace, and everything appears radiant with His presence. In such moments, you wonder how you could ever forget Him again. Yet before long the brightness seems to fade. You find yourself caught once more in anxious thoughts, old temptations, and familiar struggles. Do not be troubled by this. I was shown that such seasons belong to this earthly life. We are given glimpses of God's nearness to strengthen our hope, but we are not yet able to live continually in that vision. Our deepest joy still lies ahead.

When the light withdraws, it is easy to imagine that God has withdrawn as well. Yet this is not so. What changes is not His love but your awareness of it. Left to yourself, you begin to see only your weakness. You notice your fears, your failures, and the unrest within your own heart. The soul that has lost sight of God easily becomes preoccupied with itself. This is the root of much of our sorrow. If we could behold Him without interruption, the power of sin would lose its hold, for His presence is the healing of every divided desire.

As I reflected on this, I found five movements stirring within my heart. There was joy because I had truly encountered God. There was sorrow because I could not yet remain in that vision. There was longing to know Him more deeply. There was a gentle fear that I might lose what I had been given. And beneath all these was a steadfast hope that His mercy would never fail me. It was this hope that carried me. Though the vision passed, His promise remained. Though my feelings changed, His keeping did not.

So when you find yourself in a season of dryness, do not conclude that you have failed or that God has hidden His face from you in displeasure. Continue to seek Him with quiet faith. Let longing become your companion rather than your enemy. Every return to prayer, every small act of trust, every whispered cry for help draws your heart once again toward the One who has never ceased holding you. One day the veil will fall away forever, and the glimpses that now come and go will give way to unending communion. Until then, receive both the moments of sweetness and the seasons of waiting with humility and hope. The God who once revealed His love to you will reveal it again, and at last you shall behold Him without end.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *When I cannot see You...*

> **Exhale:** *...You still hold me.*
