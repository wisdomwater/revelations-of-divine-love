## Day 19 – You Are My Heaven

> *Heaven is not first a place to reach, but a Person to whom you belong.*

As I remained beneath the cross, every part of me longed for relief. My eyes wished to look elsewhere, to escape the sorrow before me, yet I knew that my safest place was to remain where Christ was. Even in His suffering, I found no desire to turn away from Him. Then a quiet thought arose within me, inviting me to lift my gaze toward heaven itself. But another answer sprang up from a place deeper than thought, and I replied with all the strength my soul possessed: "No. You are my Heaven." In that moment I understood that I desired not a place without pain, but the One whose love had captured my heart.

This is the secret I long for you to discover as well. It is possible to seek peace more than the Presence from which peace flows. We can begin to long for answers, blessings, or even heaven itself while forgetting that every true gift finds its life in Christ. Yet if you have Him, nothing essential is missing. Whether your days are filled with joy or overshadowed by sorrow, your deepest home is not waiting somewhere in the future. It is found in the One who is already nearer to you than your own breath.

I also learned something tender about my own weakness. Part of me recoiled from the suffering I had witnessed. My body and emotions wished they had never asked to see such things. Yet beneath that trembling was another, deeper part of my soul that never wavered. There I found a quiet, steadfast yes that belonged not to fear but to love. I realized that these two voices often live within us. One shrinks back because it is weary and vulnerable. The other has already begun to rest in God. Do not condemn yourself when the weaker voice cries out. God knows the frailty of your humanity far better than you do.

Trust that the deepest part of your life is being gently drawn toward Christ, even when your feelings resist the journey. Grace is patient. It does not force the frightened heart but lovingly gathers it until every part of you is united in love. One day there will be no division within you. The fears of the flesh and the longing of the spirit will become one song of joy. Until that day, return again and again to this simple confession: "You are my Heaven." If you can hold to that, through comfort and through trial alike, you are already walking the road that leads home.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You are my Heaven.*

> Exhale: *I rest in You.*
