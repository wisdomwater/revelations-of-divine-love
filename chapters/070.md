## Day 70 – Keep Yourself in Faith

> *When the light fades from your sight, let it remain alive in your trust.*

There are moments when God seems wonderfully near. His love becomes so clear that doubt falls silent, and your heart rests without effort. Yet such moments are gifts, not possessions. They come, and they pass. I was shown from the beginning that the vision itself would not remain. What would remain was something deeper: faith. The Lord did not leave me with outward proof that I could hold in my hands. He left me with His promise. He invited me not to cling to the experience, but to cling to Him. There is a great difference between the two. Experiences brighten the path for a time, but faith carries you when the path grows dark.

When the vision first passed from me, I believed I had lost everything. In my weakness I even dismissed what I had seen, thinking it had been nothing more than imagination. Yet our gracious Lord did not leave me in that despair. With great tenderness He reminded me that what He had shown me was true. He did not scold me for my weakness. He simply invited me to believe again. His mercy is like this. When your heart forgets what it once knew, He patiently teaches it once more. He does not tire of leading you back to the truth. Again and again He whispers, "Receive this. Believe it. Keep yourself within it. Let it comfort you. Trust it."

Faith is not pretending to see what you do not see. Faith is remembering what Love has already revealed, even when your feelings cannot confirm it. There will always be voices that challenge your trust. Some arise from your own fears and uncertainties. Others come from the wounds you have carried or the trials you now endure. There are seasons when everything within you seems to question God's goodness. Do not be discouraged by this struggle. A faith that has never been tested has not yet learned how deeply it can rest in God. Every trial becomes an invitation to discover that His faithfulness is stronger than your uncertainty.

The Lord teaches each soul in ways suited to its own journey. Sometimes He teaches through joy. Sometimes through silence. Sometimes through unexpected beauty, and sometimes through waiting. However He comes, welcome Him with an open heart. Receive His presence gently rather than grasping for certainty. Hold His words faithfully even when they seem to disappear beneath the ordinary demands of life. The treasure He gives you is not meant to flash brightly for a moment and then be forgotten. It is meant to become the quiet foundation upon which your whole life is built.

I have come to understand that there is no safer place for the soul than faith. Beyond it, while we still walk this earthly road, there is nothing more secure to attain. Beneath it, there is no lasting refuge to be found. So remain within this holy trust. Let it become the home of your heart. When questions arise, let faith hold them. When sorrow comes, let faith shelter you. When joy overflows, let faith deepen your gratitude. For the One who first awakened your trust will also sustain it to the end. And as you remain in Him, you will discover that faith itself is already a participation in the life of the God who never ceases to keep you.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *I trust Your promise.*

> Exhale: *Keep me in faith.*
