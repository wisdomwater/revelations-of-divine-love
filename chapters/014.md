## Day 14 – Every Act of Love Is Remembered

> *Nothing offered in love is ever forgotten.*

After these things were shown to me, I heard the Lord speak words that filled my heart with quiet wonder: **"I thank thee for thy travail."** I had expected instruction, perhaps comfort, but I did not expect gratitude. Yet this is the astonishing courtesy of God. The One who gives us every breath also delights to thank those who have loved Him through the ordinary faithfulness of their lives. No act of willing love passes unnoticed. No quiet sacrifice disappears into silence. The heart that turns toward God, however imperfectly, is received with joy.

Then my understanding was lifted toward heaven, where I saw not a distant ruler seated upon a throne, but a gracious host filling His house with gladness. His presence was itself the feast. Every face was bright with joy because His own countenance shone upon them with endless delight. I understood that heaven is not merely a place where sorrow has ended. It is the home where divine love welcomes every soul as a beloved friend. There is no envy there, no striving to be greater than another, only the shared happiness of being gathered into the life of God.

I was also shown that every willing act of service is remembered. Not because God needs to keep a record, but because love delights to honor love. Even the smallest offering made with a sincere heart is treasured forever. There are countless moments in your life that no one else has seen: the quiet prayer whispered in weariness, the kindness that received no thanks, the forgiveness that cost you dearly, the faithful step taken when no reward seemed possible. None of these are lost. Divine love remembers what the world forgets, and heaven rejoices over every heart that has chosen love.

So do not measure your life by what others notice or applaud. Measure it by the willingness with which you have offered it to God. Whether you have walked with Him for many years or have only just begun to turn your heart toward Him, His welcome is full and His gratitude is sincere. Love counts differently than the world does. It treasures willingness more than achievement and faithfulness more than greatness. Live each day as a quiet offering, trusting that the God who sees in secret will one day reveal that nothing given in love was ever small.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You remember love.*
>
> Exhale: *I offer You my life.*
