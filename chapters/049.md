## Day 49 – God Is Your Peace

> *Where God is welcomed, peace quietly takes the place of fear.*

One truth returned to me again and again until it settled deep within my soul: God is not divided against Himself. He does not move between love and anger as though His heart were uncertain. His very being is love, and from that love your life has always been held. If He were anything less than perfectly good, none of us could remain for even a moment. Your existence itself is sustained by His endless kindness. You were created in love, you are kept in love, and you are being drawn ever more deeply into that same love.

The unrest you experience does not begin in God. It begins within the divided places of your own heart. Fear struggles against trust. Pride resists humility. Self-protection competes with surrender. These inner conflicts become the source of so much of your suffering. Yet the Lord does not stand at a distance while you wrestle with them. He surrounds your turmoil with His gentleness. Even when you cannot find peace within yourself, He remains your peace. His steady presence quietly holds together what feels as though it might come apart.

Little by little, His mercy teaches your heart to become like His own. As you receive His kindness, your anger softens. As you experience His patience, you become more patient with yourself and with others. As His love takes root, your soul begins to discover a deeper harmony. Peace is not merely the absence of conflict. It is the quiet wholeness that grows when every part of your life is gathered into the love of God. In that peace, the soul is united with Him, for there is nothing in Him that opposes love.

Do not imagine that your present struggles are meaningless. The Lord showed me that even the inner battles which now bring sorrow will one day become part of your joy. Nothing endured in love is ever wasted. Every fear surrendered to Him, every act of forgiveness, every moment you choose peace instead of resentment is being gathered into His eternal work. What now feels heavy will one day be revealed as glory. What now seems broken will become a testimony to His faithfulness. So let your heart rest in this assurance: God Himself is your peace. He is keeping you even when you cannot keep yourself, and He will not cease His gentle work until every trace of unrest has been transformed into everlasting joy.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *You are my peace.*

> **Exhale:** *My heart rests in You.*
