## Day 9 – We Belong to One Another

> *Love grows larger each time it forgets itself.*

Do not imagine that these revelations were given because I was more beloved than anyone else. If they have any worth, it is only because they help me love God more deeply. And if they help you love God more deeply, then they belong as much to you as they ever did to me. I have never believed that God's love is measured by extraordinary experiences. Many souls who have never seen what I have seen love Him more faithfully than I do. The greatest gift is not a vision, but a heart awakened to love.

When I look only at myself, I quickly discover how little I am. Yet this does not lead me into discouragement, for I was never created to stand alone. I belong to the great fellowship of all who are being drawn into the love of God. There, my life is joined to countless others, each one cherished by the same divine heart. Love frees us from the lonely burden of comparing ourselves with one another. Instead, we begin to rejoice whenever grace flourishes in another soul, knowing that what blesses one member of God's family enriches us all.

As my understanding deepened, I saw that the love of God is never narrow. The One who made all things also loves all that He has made. Whenever your heart widens in compassion toward another person, you are beginning to see with God's own eyes. Every act of mercy, every word of kindness, every quiet forgiveness participates in a love far greater than your own. You do not draw closer to God by turning away from others. You discover Him more fully as your heart learns to embrace them.

There are mysteries that cannot be carried by words alone. I have spoken as faithfully as I am able, yet every sentence falls short of the reality itself. Some truths can only be known as the Spirit gently awakens them within you. So receive these words with an open and trusting heart. Do not seek merely to understand them with your mind. Allow them to become a quiet place where God Himself may meet you. I believe He will reveal His love to you in ways more personal and more beautiful than anything I could ever describe.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *We are one in love.*
>
> Exhale: *Draw us closer to You.*
