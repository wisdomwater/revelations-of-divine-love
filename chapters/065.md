## Day 65 – One Love, One Heart

> *The love that holds you also holds every soul.*

I have been shown that whoever turns toward God with a sincere heart is already being held by a love that has no end. Your love for God did not begin this relationship; it is your awakening to One who has loved you from before your first breath. Because His love is everlasting, your hope need not rise and fall with your feelings. There will be days when faith seems bright and joyful, and there will be days when it feels hidden beneath clouds. Yet His love remains unchanged. He desires that even now you begin to live with the quiet assurance that one day will become perfect sight. Hope is not merely waiting for heaven. It is learning to rest today in the certainty of God's faithful heart.

As this assurance grows within you, let it be joined with humility. The nearer I come to God, the more I see both His immeasurable greatness and my own smallness. Yet this does not make me feel forgotten. It fills me with wonder. The vastness of God does not diminish me; it becomes the place where I am most safely held. This holy reverence is not the fear of a servant trembling before a harsh master. It is the joyful awe of one who stands before infinite goodness and discovers that such greatness is also infinite tenderness. The soul that knows this finds both peace and delight, for love has cast out every fear except the sweet desire never to wander from the One who loves so completely.

I was also shown something that changed the way I see every other person. You are to receive God's love as though everything Christ has done was done personally for you. Not because you alone are loved, but because divine love is so complete that it gives itself wholly to each soul without being divided. And as you receive this love, you begin to understand that no one stands outside it. The same mercy that embraces you reaches toward every person you meet. Love joins what fear would separate. It teaches you that another's joy is not apart from your own, and another's sorrow is not entirely their burden to carry. In God's charity we are not isolated lives, but members of one living body, sustained by one eternal Love.

Because this is true, do not give your deepest fear to anything except the loss of love itself. The powers that once seemed so threatening have already been placed into the hands of Christ, your faithful Friend. Much of what frightens you belongs not to eternal reality but to passing weakness, anxious thoughts, or the frailty of the body. These things are real, but they do not have the final word. Whenever fear rises within you, gently lift your gaze again toward the One who holds all things. The more deeply you know Him, the more quietly your soul will come to rest. Peace grows wherever trust takes root.

These revelations have carried me from suffering into hope, from questioning into wonder, and from fear into the certainty of love. If there is one thread woven through everything I have been shown, it is this: God desires that you know how completely you are loved. Let this assurance become the place from which you live. Receive His love as though it were given entirely for you, and then offer that same love freely to all whom He has made. In this way, you will begin to discover even now the unity and joy that await us in their fullness, where every soul will rejoice together in the endless love of God.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *Your love unites us.*

> **Exhale:** *I rest in Your peace.*
