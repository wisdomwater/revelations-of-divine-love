## Day 44 – Made for Love

> *Your soul finds its truest life when it sees, beholds, and loves God.*

There is a deep purpose woven into your very being. You were not created merely to exist, to strive, or to pass through this life searching for meaning. You were made for God. The Lord showed me that the soul naturally moves toward its Maker, just as a flower turns toward the light. This is the work for which you were created, and it is a work that never ends: to behold God, to delight in God, and to rest in His love. Every longing of your heart is, beneath its many disguises, a longing to return to this simple joy.

I was shown that the soul comes alive through three beautiful movements. First, it sees the truth of God. Then it lingers in quiet wonder, contemplating His wisdom. From these springs the sweetest gift of all: love. Love is not separate from truth or wisdom, but their joyful flowering. The more clearly you perceive God's goodness, the more naturally your heart begins to love Him. This is why the deepest knowledge is never cold or merely intellectual. True seeing always awakens love, and love opens your eyes to see even more clearly.

These same qualities are found perfectly in God Himself. He is endless Truth, endless Wisdom, and endless Love. Yet in His generosity He has planted reflections of these gifts within you. They are not yours by nature as they are His, but they have been lovingly given to you so that your life might share in His own. Whenever you seek what is true, cherish what is good, or choose the way of love, you are living according to the deepest design of your soul. You are becoming more fully who you were created to be.

As you behold the greatness of God, you may sometimes feel very small. I have felt this wonder myself. Yet this is not the smallness of shame but of joyful awe. The more clearly you see His majesty, the more deeply you discover His tenderness. The God who is beyond all imagining delights to keep you always within His love. There you find your true dignity—not in making much of yourself, but in belonging to the One who made you. Let your days become a quiet practice of seeing, beholding, and loving. For this is the life of heaven already beginning within you, and the joy for which your soul has always been created.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Open my eyes to You.*

> Exhale: *Fill my heart with love.*
