## Day 46 – The God Who Is Never Other Than Love

> *The clearer you see God's goodness, the less you fear His heart.*

As you journey through this life, you are learning who you truly are. Much of what you call yourself is only the surface—the changing thoughts, emotions, successes, and failures that rise and fall with each passing day. Yet beneath these passing experiences is your truest self, held securely in God from the beginning. You will not know that self completely until every shadow has passed away and you stand before Him in everlasting joy. Until then, let your longing to know God become also a longing to know the person He has always seen within you.

Do not be afraid to acknowledge your sin. It is good and necessary to see where you have wounded love, where you have failed to do the good you were called to do, and where your life still longs for healing. Such honesty is not the enemy of peace. It is the doorway to it. A heart that refuses to see its need cannot receive mercy. Yet as you become more aware of your weakness, do not imagine that God has become less loving toward you. The clearer your vision of yourself becomes, the clearer your vision of His compassion must become also.

I was shown something that astonished me and has remained with me ever since. Though I knew well that sin deserves sorrow and that we stand in need of grace, I never saw the Lord filled with wrath toward His beloved children. I saw Him as He truly is: Goodness, Life, Truth, Love, and Peace. These are not qualities God sometimes possesses. They are His very being. Love cannot cease to be love. Goodness cannot become its opposite. The One who created you does not move toward you with changing moods or wounded pride. He moves toward you with the unwavering constancy of perfect love. Nothing can separate your deepest self from the God in whom your life is held.

There are mysteries here that cannot yet be fully understood, and I gladly leave them in God's wise keeping. Not every question has been answered for me, nor need every mystery be solved before your heart can rest. What has been shown is enough for today: God calls you to truth without abandoning you to fear. He invites you to repentance without withdrawing His embrace. So come to Him as a trusting child. Let His goodness teach you to be honest, and let His love teach you not to despair. The nearer you draw to Him, the more you will discover that He has been drawing near to you all along. In that meeting place, truth and mercy are no longer enemies, but companions leading you into the fullness of joy.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You are endless Love.*

> Exhale: *I rest without fear.*
