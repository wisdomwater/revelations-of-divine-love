## Day 84 – In the End, All Shall Be Love

> *The light that guides you now is the love that will one day fill all things.*

I was shown that the light God gives you in this life is measured with perfect wisdom. It is not so bright that every mystery is removed, nor so dim that you are left without hope. He gives exactly the light your soul needs for this day's journey. There are times when you wish you could see the whole path stretching before you, but love knows better. If everything were revealed at once, there would be little room for trust. So our gracious Lord leads you gently, giving enough light for faithful steps while keeping before you the promise of a greater dawn yet to come.

Do not think, then, that your labor of love goes unnoticed. Every quiet act of faithfulness, every hidden prayer, every kindness offered without recognition, every burden patiently carried for love's sake is precious to God. I heard Him speak words of gratitude that astonished me: **"I thank you for your service and your labor."** Imagine such humility in the heart of God—that the One who gives you every strength should also delight in thanking you for using it. Nothing done in love is ever forgotten. Love sees what the world overlooks and treasures what others never notice.

I came to understand that this guiding light is charity—that holy love which has its source in God Himself. It is love that keeps faith alive when certainty is impossible. It is love that gives hope the courage to continue. Faith and hope are faithful companions for this earthly pilgrimage, but both are carried by something greater. Love holds them together. Faith believes because love is trustworthy. Hope endures because love has promised. And when every journey is complete, faith will become sight, hope will be fulfilled, but love will remain forever. Truly, in the end, all shall be love.

I was shown three beautiful ways of understanding this love. First, there is the love that has no beginning and no end: this is God Himself. Second, there is the love by which your own soul was created to live in God, bearing His likeness like a mirror turned toward the sun. Third, there is the love He places within you as a living gift, enabling you to love Him, to receive His love for yourself, and to love every person He has made. This love is not something you manufacture. It is His own life quietly flowering within you.

Therefore, let your heart rest in this simple truth. The light that has guided you through every darkness has always been love. The strength that has carried you through every weakness has always been love. The home toward which your whole life is moving is nothing less than love itself. Walk, then, in the light you have been given today. Love God for His own sake. Receive His love with gratitude. Love yourself as one dearly cherished by Him. And let that same love overflow toward every soul you meet. For this is the beginning of the eternal life that will one day fill heaven and earth, when every shadow has passed away and all things are gathered forever into the endless charity of God.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Fill me with Your love.*

> Exhale: *Let love be my light.*
