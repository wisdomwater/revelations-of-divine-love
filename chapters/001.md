## Day 1 – Love Is the Beginning

> *Every true vision begins with love.*

I want you to know, before we go any further, what these revelations truly are. They are not merely visions of suffering or mysteries beyond understanding. They are revelations of divine love. Everything I have seen flows from that single source. If you remember nothing else as we journey together, remember this: love is the beginning from which every vision unfolds.

You will hear of Christ's Passion, His wounds, the struggle against evil, the mystery of prayer, the hope of heaven, and the hidden ways God works in every soul. At times these things may seem difficult to understand. Yet do not let your heart become troubled. Each revelation is bound together by the same unchanging love. Even where sorrow appears, love is already at work.

I have learned that we often seek answers before we have learned to trust. We want every mystery explained before we are willing to rest in God's care. Yet love asks something different of us. It invites us to begin with confidence in God's goodness, even when we cannot yet see the whole picture. Understanding grows slowly, but trust can begin today.

So come with an open heart. There will be mysteries we cannot fully grasp and questions that remain for another day. Yet if you allow love to become the light by which you see, you will discover that every step, whether joyful or painful, leads more deeply into the heart of God.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Love leads me.*
>
> Exhale: *I will follow.*
