## Day 31 – The Longing of Love

> *The heart of God is always reaching toward you.*

There is a longing within God that you may not have noticed before. You have likely imagined yourself as the one searching, praying, and reaching toward heaven, hoping to draw near to the One you love. Yet I was shown something even more wonderful. Before your heart ever stirred toward God, His heart was already turned toward you. His desire for you did not begin when you first believed, nor when you first called His name. It has existed from all eternity. Love has always been moving toward you, patiently drawing you home.

When fears arise and questions trouble your mind, remember what the Lord spoke with such quiet certainty: *I may make all things well. I can make all things well. I will make all things well. I shall make all things well. You yourself shall see that all manner of thing shall be well.* Let these words become a place of rest. They are not merely promises about the future but windows into the very life of God. The Father holds every possibility. The Son has accomplished every necessary work. The Holy Spirit faithfully carries that work into your life. The blessed Trinity is united in one unwavering purpose: to bring you into everlasting joy. Rest your soul there, where divine love never hesitates and never fails.

The thirst of Christ is not only the thirst He knew upon the cross. It is the everlasting desire of Love that refuses to abandon what it has made. His suffering revealed outwardly what had always lived within His heart. He longs for every one of His children to be gathered into the fullness of life. Until all that can be healed is healed, until every soul has reached the joy prepared for it, His love continues to reach outward. This longing is not born from lack, for God lacks nothing. It springs instead from perfect goodness, which delights in giving itself away. His joy is made complete as His children discover the joy for which they were created.

If you find within yourself a longing for God that cannot be satisfied by anything this world offers, do not mistake it for your own work alone. Your desire is the quiet echo of His desire for you. Every prayer, every hope, every restless turning of your heart has already been anticipated by the One who has never ceased calling you to Himself. So do not grow discouraged if the journey feels unfinished. Love is patient because Love sees the end from the beginning. Trust the gentle pull upon your soul. The One who longs for you more deeply than you can imagine will not cease drawing you until you are wholly at home in the everlasting bliss for which you were lovingly made.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *You long for me.*

> **Exhale:** *I come to You.*
