## Day 62 – The Home from Which You Came

> *The One who gave you life is also the One who is gently leading you home.*

I have been shown that there is no season of your life in which God holds you less carefully than another. When your heart is filled with joy, He is near. When sorrow overshadows your days, He is no less near. When you feel strong in faith, His love surrounds you. And when you feel weak, forgotten, or cast aside, He keeps you with the very same tenderness. Nothing you endure is wasted. Love gathers every grief, every disappointment, every hidden struggle, and quietly weaves it into the story of your salvation. There is no lost time with God. Even those seasons that seem empty to you are being transformed into something that will one day shine with unexpected beauty.

I was also shown something deeper still. God is not merely the giver of life. He is the very source from which all life continually flows. Every goodness you recognize in creation has its beginning in Him. Every kindness, every beauty, every truth, every longing for love has its root in the One who is Goodness itself. He is both Father and Mother, the eternal origin of all that exists and the faithful love that continually sustains it. You have never stood outside this love. From the first breath of creation until this very moment, your life has rested within the life of God.

Because you have come from Him, your deepest desire has always been to return to Him. This longing is woven into your very nature. Often you search for fulfillment in distant places, believing that peace lies just beyond the next achievement, the next possession, or the next certainty. Yet the truest journey is not outward but inward. Do not search endlessly for God as though He were hidden in some far-off place. Turn gently toward the quiet sanctuary within your own soul, where Christ has already made His dwelling. There, beneath the noise of fear and striving, you will begin to discover the One who has been waiting for you all along. What is now glimpsed by faith will one day be seen face to face in unending joy.

Do not imagine that this promise belongs only to a chosen few or to those who believe themselves more worthy than others. What I have seen is far too generous to be possessed by any one person. The beauty of our humanity was fashioned for Christ, and in Him it is offered to us all. Whatever dignity, nobility, or glory belongs to humanity is first His, and through His grace it becomes ours as well. We are not isolated souls competing for God's affection. We belong together, gathered into the life of the One who has loved us from before the beginning of time.

So live today with quiet confidence. You did not create yourself, and you do not carry yourself alone. The Love that called you into being continues to sustain you with every breath. The path before you may still hold mystery, but its destination is already secure. The God who is your beginning is also your ending. The home for which your heart has always longed is not a distant place you must earn. It is the embrace of the One from whom you came and to whom you are forever being drawn.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You are my beginning.*

> Exhale: *You are my home.*
