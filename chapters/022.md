## Day 22 – Love Beyond Measure

> *Love is never exhausted by what it gives.*

As I looked upon Christ, He asked me a question that reached deeper than my thoughts: "Are you pleased that I suffered for you?" My heart could answer only with gratitude. Then He spoke words that filled me with astonishment: "If you are pleased, I am pleased." I began to understand that His sacrifice was never offered reluctantly. Love did not drag Him to the cross. Love carried Him there willingly. What brought Him joy was not the suffering itself, but the healing it would bring to those He loved. His delight has always been your restoration.

Then I was lifted into a vision of heaven, and there I saw that the deepest joy of Christ is not found in glory separated from us, but in sharing that glory with us. I was shown that we are His delight, His reward, His crown. Imagine such a thing. We spend so much of our lives wondering whether we are enough for God, while Christ rejoices that we belong to Him. The love that created you has never regarded you as an afterthought. You are cherished beyond your own understanding. Every step He has taken toward your salvation has been taken with joy.

Then I heard words that no human heart could ever invent: "If I might suffer more, I would suffer more." I did not hear this as a desire for pain, but as the limitless language of love. Love is always willing to give itself. If there had been a greater way to reveal His heart, He would have embraced it. The cross was not the measure of how much suffering God desired. It was the measure of how completely He refused to let anything stand between Himself and the beloved. His love cannot be increased because it has never known limits.

So when you are tempted to wonder whether God truly loves you, do not begin by measuring your own worthiness. Begin instead by contemplating His heart. Before there was a cross, there was love. After the cross, there remains love. The suffering of Christ was a moment in time, but the love from which it flowed has neither beginning nor end. Rest your soul there. Every kindness you receive, every mercy that surprises you, every quiet whisper of grace is another expression of the same endless love that chose you before you ever knew how to choose Him. Nothing can enlarge that love, and nothing can diminish it. It is the everlasting home in which your life is already held.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Your love is endless.*

> Exhale: *I rest in Your delight.*
