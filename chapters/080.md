## Day 80 – You Never Leave Him Alone

> *Even when you forget God, He never forgets you.*

I have been shown that God lovingly guides your life in more ways than you often notice. He speaks through the reason He has placed within you. He teaches through the wisdom handed down in the fellowship of His people. And He works quietly within your heart by His Holy Spirit. These are not three separate voices competing for your attention. They are one gracious God leading you in different ways toward the same home. Learn to cherish each of these gifts. They are like the first letters a child learns before reading a great book. What you understand now may seem small, but it is enough to guide your steps until the day when all things become clear in the light of heaven.

I was also shown that Christ Himself accomplishes every work necessary for your salvation. He came near by taking your humanity. He carried your healing through His life, His death, and His resurrection. And He continues His work even now. He has not entrusted your soul to another and stepped away. He remains nearer than anyone else could ever be. If there were only one soul left to be gathered into His kingdom, He would remain faithfully beside that one until the journey was complete. Such is the tenderness of His love. He is the highest Lord over all creation, yet also the nearest companion of every heart that calls upon Him.

There are moments when you feel sorrow for your distance from God. There are tears of repentance, quiet grief over your failures, and a longing to be more fully united with Him. I was shown that these holy desires do not begin with you. They are already the life of Christ stirring within your soul. Love cannot see suffering without compassion. Christ bears your longing with you. He carries your grief even before you have found words to express it. Whenever your heart aches for deeper communion with God, it is because God is already drawing you toward Himself from within.

Yet there are also times when you forget Him. You become distracted by fear, discouraged by failure, or simply weary from the ordinary burdens of life. You may imagine that in those moments Christ has withdrawn. But I was shown the opposite. Even when you leave Him waiting, He never leaves you. He continues to watch over your soul with unwavering patience. He quietly bears the whole burden of your keeping until you are ready to turn again toward Him. His love is never offended into absence. It remains steadfast, shielding you with mercy even while your heart wanders.

Therefore, whenever you become aware that you have drifted, do not delay your return. Turn back with the confidence of one who is already expected and already loved. You are not interrupting God's peace by coming to Him. You are answering the invitation He has never ceased extending. He has stood beside you through every moment, excusing your weakness with tenderness and protecting you with a love stronger than your failures. You have never truly been alone, because He has never ceased to be with you. And as you walk beside Him once more, you will discover that the One you thought you had left behind has, in truth, been faithfully walking beside you all along.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *You never leave me.*

> **Exhale:** *I return to You.*
