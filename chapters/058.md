## Day 58 – Your Life Hidden in the Trinity

> *You were created by love, restored by mercy, and are being completed by grace.*

Before the world was made, it was already God's delight to make humanity. You were never an afterthought, nor the result of chance. From the beginning, the Holy Trinity desired that you should share in the life of divine love. When God brought you into being, He did more than create you—He joined you to Himself. That holy union has never been forgotten by God, even when it has been forgotten by you. Beneath every wandering and every sorrow, there remains the quiet truth that your deepest life is still held within the One who first imagined you in love.

I was shown that the whole life of God reaches toward us in ways our hearts can begin to understand. The mighty strength of God is our Father, faithfully giving us our being and keeping us secure. The eternal Wisdom of God is our Mother, lovingly bringing us to birth, nurturing us, restoring what has been wounded, and patiently raising us into wholeness. The boundless Love of God is our Lord, drawing us onward with kindness beyond all deserving. These are not three different loves but one endless love, meeting us in every season of our journey.

The love between God and the soul is more intimate than words can express. I heard within my heart the quiet assurance: *I love you, and you love Me, and our love shall never be divided.* Even when your feelings change, this deeper covenant remains. There are days when your heart burns with gratitude, and there are days when prayer feels empty. Yet beneath both lies a love that neither begins nor ends with your emotions. God remains faithful to the union He has made. He is not searching for reasons to withdraw from you. He delights to remain with the one He calls beloved.

I saw that all our life unfolds in three gifts. First comes **Nature**, by which we receive our very existence. Then comes **Mercy**, by which Christ meets us in our weakness, heals what has been broken, and patiently restores us whenever we fall. Finally comes **Grace**, which does more than repair—it fulfills. Grace is God's generous gift that carries us beyond what we could ever earn, completing His beautiful work within us. We begin in love, we are sustained by mercy, and we are brought to fullness by grace. This is the rhythm of the whole spiritual life.

Our Lord Jesus, who is our Mother in mercy, never grows weary of forming His children. As a loving mother nourishes the life growing within her, so Christ continually nourishes your soul. He gathers together every fractured part of your life until what once seemed divided is made one again. Nothing that belongs to you is beneath His care. He patiently joins your weakness to His strength, your sorrow to His joy, and your mortality to His endless life. This quiet work often escapes your notice, yet it never ceases.

Carry this assurance into every part of your day. Your life is not held together by your own effort but by the faithful love of the Father, the tender mercy of the Son, and the generous grace of the Holy Spirit. You have your being in God, your healing in Christ, and your fulfillment in the Spirit. Rest often in this mystery. The One who created you continues to sustain you, restore you, and gently draw you toward the fullness of joy that has been prepared for you from the very beginning.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *Create, restore, fulfill me.*

> **Exhale:** *I rest in Your love.*
