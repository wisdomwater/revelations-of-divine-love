## Day 86 – Love Was the Meaning

> *At the heart of every mystery, you will always find love.*

As these revelations drew toward their close, I knew that what had been given to me could never be exhausted by words. What I have written is only the beginning of a conversation that God desires to continue within every willing heart. The Lord does not reveal Himself merely to satisfy your curiosity. He reveals Himself to awaken your love. Therefore, continue to pray. Continue to seek. Continue to give thanks, to trust, and to rejoice. Every sincere turning of your heart toward God is already held within His own gracious work within you. Even your desire to pray begins in Him, for He is the very ground from which every holy longing springs.

For many years I carried these revelations within me, wondering what their deepest meaning might be. I returned to them again and again, asking the Lord to show me what held them all together. At last the answer came with a simplicity so beautiful that it seemed to gather every vision into a single light. The Lord asked me, "Would you know My meaning?" Then He answered His own question. **Love was His meaning.** Who revealed these things? Love. What was revealed? Love. Why were they revealed? For love. I understood then that I would never discover another meaning beyond this one, because there is none greater. Every truth I had seen, every mystery I had pondered, every promise I had received was held within this endless reality.

I also saw with great certainty that before God ever created you, He already loved you. His love did not begin when you first believed. It did not awaken when you first prayed. It did not grow because of your faithfulness, nor diminish because of your failures. It simply is. From everlasting to everlasting, God is love. Everything He has made has come forth from that love. Everything He permits is ultimately gathered into that love. Everything He is doing in your life, even now, is leading you more deeply into that love. Your life had a beginning, but the love that called you into being never did.

If you remember nothing else from all that I have shared, remember this. When life becomes confusing, return to love. When sorrow overshadows your joy, return to love. When questions remain unanswered, return to love. When you fail and rise again, return to love. Love is not merely one attribute of God among many. It is the meaning that gives every other truth its light. If you hold yourself there, patiently and faithfully, you will continue learning throughout all eternity. There will never come a day when you have reached the end of God's love, for the One who is infinite will forever draw you deeper into Himself.

So I leave you where the Lord left me: not with a system to master, nor with every mystery explained, but with a heart resting in Love. Let this become the place where you live. Let it shape the way you see God, yourself, and every person you meet. Trust that the Love which knew you before the world began is the Love that carries you now and will welcome you home. And when at last you behold Him face to face, you will discover that every revelation, every longing, every joy, every sorrow, and every step of your journey has been leading you into the same eternal truth:

**Love was His meaning.**

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Love is Your meaning.*

> Exhale: *Love shall be mine.*
