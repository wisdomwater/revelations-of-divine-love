## Day 25 – See How You Are Loved

> *To behold the beauty of another soul is to glimpse the generosity of God's love.*

With great joy the Lord turned my attention to His blessed Mother and gently asked, "Would you like to see her?" I heard in His voice not merely an invitation, but delight. It was as though He were eager to share with me someone He loved beyond words. I understood that after Himself, there was no greater joy He could have shown me. The love He has for Mary is full, pure, and radiant, and He rejoices whenever that love is shared. Love does not cling possessively to what it treasures. Love delights when others also discover its beauty.

Then I understood something even more tender. He was not showing me Mary only so that I might admire her. He wished me to understand my own place within His heart. It was as though He quietly said, "Look at how I have loved her, and know that this is the kind of love I desire for you as well." In honoring her, He was revealing the generosity of divine love itself. God does not delight in lifting one soul high by leaving another behind. The glory He gives to one becomes an invitation of hope for all. The love poured into Mary's life reveals the abundance of the love that is waiting to fill every willing heart.

Yet I found that my deepest longing was not simply to behold her outwardly, but to know the beauty that had formed her soul. I desired her trust, her humility, her wisdom, and her quiet surrender to God. These are treasures that cannot fade with time. They are the true radiance of a life wholly given to love. As I looked upon her, I did not feel distant from her holiness. Instead, I felt gently drawn toward the same path of openness that allowed God's grace to flourish so beautifully within her.

So whenever you see goodness, holiness, or beauty in another person, do not let envy arise within you. Rejoice with God. Let their light become an encouragement rather than a comparison. The Lord delights in sharing His beloved with His beloved. He is always inviting you to celebrate the grace He has poured into others because His love is never scarce. The same Love that formed such beauty in Mary is patiently at work within you. Look upon her with gratitude, and let her life remind you of the astonishing tenderness with which God is shaping every soul that opens itself to His love.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Show me Your love.*

> Exhale: *Shape my heart in grace.*
