## Day 15 – Held in Every Season

> *The love that comforts you is the same love that carries you when all comfort disappears.*

There are moments when God's presence feels so near that peace settles over your soul like gentle light. In those moments, you wonder how you could ever fear again. Everything seems held. Your heart rests without striving, and you know, beyond words, that you are loved. Receive such moments with gratitude, but do not imagine they are the measure of God's faithfulness. They are gifts freely given, not rewards you have earned. Love comes to reassure you, not to make you dependent upon the feeling itself.

There will also be times when that sweetness fades. Your prayers may feel empty. Your heart may grow weary. You may become discouraged by your own weakness and wonder where the joy has gone. Do not believe that I was spared these seasons. I, too, was left feeling heavy, unable to find comfort except by holding quietly to faith, hope, and love, even when I could scarcely feel them within me. Yet I learned something precious: the absence of consolation is not the absence of God. Divine love has not withdrawn simply because your feelings have changed.

I was shown that these changing seasons are not signs of God's changing heart. Sometimes I was lifted into deep joy, and then, without warning, I found myself in sorrow again. Again and again the two were woven together. In joy I knew nothing could separate me from Christ's love. In sorrow I could only cry, "Lord, save me." Both prayers were welcomed. Both belonged to the same journey. The God who met me in delight was the very same God who held me when all I could offer was my need. His love remained constant, even while my experience rose and fell like the tide.

So when darkness comes, do not build your home there. Let sorrow speak, but do not allow it to convince you that it is the deepest truth about your life. Pain is real, but it is not everlasting. Joy is often hidden, but it is never destroyed. Lift your heart, even if only by the smallest act of trust. Hold fast to the promise that God's love surrounds you just as fully in your weariness as in your gladness. The day will come when every passing grief has dissolved into eternal joy, and you will see that through every season, whether you could feel Him or not, Love never once let go of you.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *You hold me always.*
>
> Exhale: *I rest beyond my feelings.*
