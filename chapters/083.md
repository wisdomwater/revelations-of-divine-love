## Day 83 – Your Light in the Night

> *Faith is the quiet light that carries you until dawn becomes sight.*

As I looked back over all that my Lord had shown me, I began to see that every vision rested upon three simple and beautiful truths. Wherever He revealed Himself, I found **Life, Love, and Light**. These are not merely gifts that God gives. They are the very character of the One who gives them. In His life I found a nearness more intimate than I had ever imagined. In His love I discovered a gentleness that never forces itself upon the soul. In His light I beheld the eternal reality that holds all things together. The more I contemplated these three, the more my whole being desired to rest in Him alone.

I also came to understand that the deepest part of you has always belonged to God. The capacity you have to know truth, to recognize goodness, and to seek what is beautiful is itself a gift planted within you by your Creator. Your reason is not meant to lead you away from God but toward Him. When it is joined with humility, it becomes another way of marveling at His wisdom. The mind reaches its greatest fulfillment not when it imagines it has mastered every mystery, but when it joyfully yields itself to the One who is the source of all truth.

Yet in this life we do not walk by full sight. We walk by faith. I was shown that faith is like a lamp carried through the darkness before sunrise. It does not reveal the whole landscape at once. It gives enough light for the next faithful step. There are nights when the road feels uncertain and the horizon disappears from view. During such seasons you may wish for the brightness of perfect understanding, yet God lovingly gives you something better for the present moment: the quiet confidence that He is with you, even when you cannot yet see where He is leading. Faith is not the absence of darkness. It is the presence of divine light within it.

Do not despise the night through which you now travel. Though it carries sorrow and unanswered questions, it is also the place where trust grows strong. Every time you choose to believe that Love is faithful when you cannot yet see its purpose, your soul becomes more deeply rooted in God. What now feels hidden is preparing you for a greater revelation. The night is not your destination. It is the path by which you are gently led toward the everlasting Day.

There will come a moment when every shadow disappears. Suddenly your eyes will be opened, and the light that has quietly guided you all along will surround you without limit. Faith will become sight. Hope will become fulfillment. The God who has been your light through every dark valley will be your endless joy forever. Until that day, walk confidently in the light you have been given. It is enough. For the light that leads you is not merely a gift from God—it is God Himself, faithfully shining within your night until morning breaks at last.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *Be my light.*

> **Exhale:** *Lead me until dawn.*
