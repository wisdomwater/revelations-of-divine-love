## Day 75 – The Day You Will Understand

> *What is hidden now will one day become another reason to praise Love.*

I have been shown that God already holds everything you truly need. Before you know your need, He knows it. Before your heart begins to long for Him, His heart is already longing for you. This surprised me more than I can say. We often speak of our thirst for God, yet I was shown that God Himself thirsts for His children. His desire is to gather every soul into the fullness of His life. Even now He is drawing us with quiet patience, never forcing, never abandoning, always inviting. The longing you feel for God is, in truth, His own longing awakening within you. Love calls to love until they meet.

I was shown that this holy longing unfolds in three beautiful ways. First, God longs for you to know Him more deeply, for love cannot flourish where He remains a stranger. He gently teaches your heart, not merely so that you may understand Him, but so that you may delight in Him. Second, He longs to bring you safely home when your earthly journey is complete, lifting you from every sorrow into His everlasting joy. Yet even this is not the end of His desire. There remains a greater fulfillment still, when all creation is made new and every soul is filled beyond all measure with the life of God. Love is never content to give only enough. It delights in overflowing.

There are gifts that God has prepared which no heart has yet fully imagined. They remain hidden, not because He is unwilling to give them, but because we are not yet able to receive them. Just as a child cannot yet understand the fullness of an inheritance prepared in love, so we cannot yet comprehend the joy that awaits us. One day His life will pour into us without limit, and we shall discover a happiness greater than anything we have ever known. Every promise will bloom. Every hope will find its answer. Every longing will be gathered into a joy that never diminishes.

Then something else will happen that fills me with quiet wonder. We shall finally understand. We shall see why God acted as He did and why He sometimes seemed to remain silent. We shall see not only the reasons for His mighty works, but also the reasons for all He lovingly permitted. What now appears tangled will be revealed as wisdom. What now feels unfinished will be shown as part of a greater beauty. This understanding will not lead us to pride, as though we had mastered God's mysteries. It will lead us to profound humility. We will stand in awe before the greatness of the Creator and the tenderness with which He has guided every moment toward love.

Until that day, let your heart remain open to holy longing. Do not be discouraged because you cannot yet see the whole picture. Your life is held within a story whose ending is already secure in the heart of God. Walk forward in trust, allowing both love and reverence to deepen within you. The more you know His goodness, the more you will marvel at His greatness. And the more you marvel at His greatness, the more your heart will be softened into humility, gratitude, and joy. For the day is surely coming when every unanswered question will become another reason to adore the Love that has been leading you all along.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Draw me to Yourself.*

> Exhale: *I trust what I cannot yet see.*
