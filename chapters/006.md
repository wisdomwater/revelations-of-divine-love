## Day 6 – Held Within Goodness

> *The love you seek is already surrounding you.*

I have learned that the heart often believes it must find the right words, the right prayers, or the right path before it can draw near to God. We gather beautiful practices and precious traditions, and these are gifts worthy of gratitude. Yet beneath them all is something even simpler. Every prayer, every act of worship, every holy remembrance finds its life in one endless reality: the goodness of God. It is not our efforts that bring us close to Him. It is His goodness that has already come near to us.

Everything that has ever helped your soul is an expression of that goodness. The life of Christ, His suffering, His resurrection, the love of His mother, the witness of the saints, the comfort of Scripture, the quiet strength of prayer—all of these are streams flowing from the same inexhaustible spring. Do not become so occupied with the gifts that you lose sight of the Giver. Let every blessing lead you back to the One whose goodness gives life to all things. There is nowhere you can turn where His love is not already waiting to meet you.

I was also shown that God's goodness does not remain far above our need. It descends into the lowest places of our lives. There is no sorrow too hidden, no weakness too ordinary, no burden too small to receive His loving attention. Just as your body is clothed and protected each day, so your whole being is wrapped within the goodness of God. You may not always feel it, just as you seldom notice the air that surrounds you, yet it never leaves you. Even when your own strength fails, divine goodness remains whole, unbroken, and nearer to you than your own breath.

So do not be afraid to ask freely for what your heart truly longs for. The deepest desire within you is not something God must overcome; it is something He Himself has planted there. Your longing for Him is already an echo of His longing for you. Until the day when every longing is fulfilled in His presence, let your life become a quiet practice of knowing and loving. The more you behold the One who made you, the more humility and compassion will grow within you, for you will discover that every soul you meet is held within the very same boundless goodness that has always been holding you.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Your goodness surrounds me.*
>
> Exhale: *I rest within Your love.*
