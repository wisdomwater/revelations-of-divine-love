## Day 43 – Prayer Unites the Soul to God

> *The deepest purpose of prayer is not to change God, but to draw you into His love.*

Prayer is more than words spoken into heaven. It is the quiet meeting place where your soul is drawn into the heart of God. Though you have always belonged to Him, there are times when fear, distraction, or sin make you feel far away. Prayer gently restores what your heart has forgotten. It reminds you that your truest home has never been anywhere but in the love of God. Each sincere prayer, however simple, is the soul turning once again toward the One who has never ceased turning toward it.

Do not imagine that prayer persuades God to become kinder than He already is. His love does not need convincing. Before you ever formed a request, He had already desired your good. He delights in your prayers because they allow your heart to join willingly in the good work He is already accomplishing. It pleased the Lord to show me that He receives every sincere prayer with joy, almost as though He were thanking us for allowing Him to give what He has long desired to give. Such is the humility of divine love. The Creator rejoices whenever His children freely say yes to the life He is offering.

There are moments when prayer becomes wonderfully simple. Sometimes the Lord draws so near that every question falls silent. You no longer know what to ask because His presence has become the answer your soul was seeking all along. Words fade into quiet wonder. You simply behold Him, and in beholding Him you discover a joy deeper than speech. Yet there are other seasons when His presence feels hidden and your heart is restless. Do not think these seasons are failures. They are invitations to seek Him again with patient love. Prayer in times of darkness is no less precious than prayer in times of light, for both keep your heart turned toward Him.

Remain faithful through every season. Whether you pray with overflowing joy or with weary perseverance, the Lord is drawing you ever closer to Himself. Little by little, through countless hidden moments of grace, He prepares your soul for the day when every longing will be fulfilled. Even now He grants gentle glimpses of His presence, as much as your heart can bear. These moments awaken an even deeper desire—not merely for answers, blessings, or peace, but for God Himself. One day that desire will reach its perfect fulfillment. You shall know yourself completely in His love, and you shall behold Him face to face, with nothing left between you. Until then, let every prayer, whether spoken or silent, become another step into the eternal embrace that has been waiting for you from the very beginning.

**Breath Prayer**

> Inhale: *Draw me into You.*

> Exhale: *I rest in Your love.*
