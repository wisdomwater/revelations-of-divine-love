## Day 35 – See God in Everything

> *The heart finds peace when it learns to seek God before it seeks answers.*

After the Lord had filled my heart with the joy of His goodness, I found myself wanting certainty about someone I dearly loved. I longed to know how their story would unfold and whether they would remain faithful. My concern was born from love, yet I discovered that my gaze had become too narrow. Instead of answering my particular question, the Lord gently drew my eyes higher. I came to understand that it is a greater honor to behold God in all things than to seek certainty about any one thing. Love is not diminished by this wider vision. It is purified by it.

How often your heart does the same. You become occupied with a single outcome, one relationship, one unanswered prayer, one fear about the future. Your thoughts circle around it until it seems to fill the whole horizon. Yet the Lord quietly invites you to lift your eyes. He is present not only in the one thing that concerns you, but in everything. His wisdom is not confined to one story. His love is not limited to one moment. When you begin to see His faithful presence woven through all of life, the burden of needing every particular answer begins to loosen its grip upon your soul.

This does not mean that suffering is good, nor that evil should be welcomed. There is much in this world that grieves the heart of God. Yet even what He permits is never beyond His redeeming love. His goodness continues its quiet work where you cannot yet see it. His mercy meets every wound. His wisdom guides every wandering path. Nothing escapes His patient care. The God who created all things has never ceased leading creation toward the fullness He intended from the beginning. Though you may not yet understand the road, you may trust the One who walks it with you.

So let your joy rest first in God Himself rather than in changing circumstances. Give thanks when particular blessings come, but do not build your peace upon them alone. Likewise, do not surrender your hope when particular sorrows arrive. Beneath every season flows the unchanging current of divine love. By mercy you are lifted whenever you fall. By grace you are carried farther than you could ever travel alone. One day you will see how every thread has been gathered into perfect harmony. Until then, practice seeing God everywhere—in moments of delight and in moments of waiting, in the ordinary as much as the extraordinary. The more you behold Him in all things, the more your heart will discover the quiet joy that nothing in this world can take away.

**Breath Prayer**

> **Inhale:** *Open my eyes.*

> **Exhale:** *I see You everywhere.*
